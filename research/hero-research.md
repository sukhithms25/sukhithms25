# Hero Section Research & Analysis

We evaluated 10 GitHub profiles with highly engaging, professional, or unique hero sections. This analysis focuses on layout, visual cues, and the immediate message conveyed to the visitor.

---

## Profile 1: m1guelpf
* **What caught your eye?** Clean, developer-centric branding. A single, short, high-impact subtitle, combined with clean social badges that don't overwhelm.
* **Color palette:** Monochromatic dark gray/white with neon blue accents.
* **Layout:** Left-aligned text header with a custom badge layout on the right.
* **Animation?** No animation; completely static but highly responsive.
* **Icons?** Minimalist, monochromatic tech/social icons.
* **Professional?** Yes, extremely. Fits a senior developer or independent hacker.
* **Too cluttered?** No, perfect amount of whitespace.
* **Would you steal this idea?** Yes, the clean layout spacing and color cohesion.
* **Inspiration Score:**
  * Hero: 9/10
  * Layout: 9/10
  * Animations: N/A
  * Professionalism: 10/10
  * Readability: 10/10
  * Creativity: 7/10
  * **Overall: 9.0/10**
